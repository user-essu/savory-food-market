Bringing Home-Cooked Goodness to Quinapondan

Savory was founded in 2025 by Elaissa Asebias Bustamante, an ESSU student from Quinapondan who missed the taste of her mother's cooking while studying. What started as a small home-based food business has grown into a beloved online food market serving the local community.

We partner with local home cooks and small food businesses in Quinapondan to bring you authentic Filipino dishes that taste like home. Every meal is prepared fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients from Eastern Samar's vibrant markets.

Our mission is simple: to provide nutritious, delicious, and affordable meals to our community while supporting local food entrepreneurs in Quinapondan and surrounding areas.
